Data Ingestion
Step 01

Raw data
ingestion &
validation.

Clinical transcriptions are loaded from the MTSamples corpus — 4,999 real-world medical notes spanning 40 specialties. The DataLoader validates schema, normalises encodings, and streams records into the SQLite store with zero data loss.

DataLoader · SQLite · Pandas
Step 02

Pre-NER
cleaning &
normalisation.

Before any entity detection, the DataCleaner strips boilerplate artifacts, normalises whitespace, corrects common OCR errors, and prepares the text surface for maximum NER recall. All transformations are logged for audit traceability.

DataCleaner · Regex · Unicode Norm
Step 03

Hybrid NER
pipeline &
ML modeling.

The NERPipeline fuses spaCy's statistical model with a hand-crafted regex layer — the hybrid approach outperforms either in isolation by +11 F1 points. Spans are extracted, confidence-scored, and returned with precise character offsets.

spaCy · IsolationForest · HybridNER
Step 04

PHI redaction
& audit
logging.

Every detected PHI span is replaced with its canonical label token (e.g. [NAME], [MRN]). Every API call, prediction, and cleaning event is written to an immutable audit log — full ICH E6 (R2) GCP traceability chain.

AuditLogger · HIPAA · EventSourcing
